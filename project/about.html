About The Regalia

Home / About
Since 1994

Three Decades of Quiet, Considered Hospitality

was built on a simple belief: that true luxury lies in craftsmanship and attentiveness, not excess. Our foundations are drawn from Bihar's own architectural and artistic traditions — hand-carved jharokha windows, terrazzo floors, and brassware sourced from local artisans — set against the comforts guests expect from an international five-star address.

What began as a single heritage wing has grown into a full destination: 120 rooms and suites, three restaurants, a spa, and banquet halls that have hosted everything from state visits to thousand-guest weddings. Through every expansion, the same family that opened our doors in 1994 continues to walk our corridors today.
