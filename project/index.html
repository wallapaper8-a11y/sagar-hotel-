Our Story

A Heritage Woven Into Every Corner

Set in the heart of BIHAR, The HERITAGEblends Bihar's storied craftsmanship with the comforts of contemporary five-star living. From hand-finished interiors to a team trained in the art of quiet, attentive service, every detail is composed rather than assembled.

For three decades, we have hosted statesmen, celebrations, and quiet getaways alike — always with the same unwavering standard of grace.
